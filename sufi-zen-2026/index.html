What is Sufi-Zen?

Sufi is the expression of the ancient wisdom of the heart — the path of love born from that wisdom. Zen is the silence, the sky, of that wisdom. Together, this unique process is a journey to silence through love, and to love through silence.

A structured process of Sufi whirling, Zen sitting, and Zen walking. Whirling takes us into the many expressions of ourselves and finally to the state of union — sitting in Zazen. Finding the stillness in our whirling, and the song, the dance in our sitting.

This camp is a powerful process of emptying ourselves — an atmosphere where love and prayer flower into meditation.

"We have burned all traces of work and profession,

Who is Videha?

Osho disciple since 1980, Videha was initiated into the Mevlevi Sufi Order of Konya — "The Whirling Dervishes." He integrates the Sufi Path into Osho's vision and love, and has been leading groups and meditation retreats for more than 35 years, uniting the path of Love and the path of Meditation.

Videha now resides in Northern Italy where he created the Osho Mokurai Meditation Center.
